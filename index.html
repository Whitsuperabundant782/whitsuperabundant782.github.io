🎛️ Initial Configuration

Once you open the software, you must link your hardware to the application. Go to the Settings menu located at the top left corner of the window. Select the Audio tab. Choose your specific audio interface from the drop-down list. This selection ensures that sound plays through your speakers or headphones. Adjust the buffer size setting if you notice audio delay during playback.

🎧 Working with MIDI and Audio

The interface uses a timeline view for all your projects. You can drag and drop sound files directly from your computer folders into the main timeline. If you use a MIDI controller, the software detects these devices automatically upon connection. Select your controller in the input settings to begin recording virtual instruments. Use the track inspector on the left side to add compressors, equalizers, or reverb effects to your individual audio tracks.
